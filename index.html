Palindrome Highlighter

Edit the text in this box to see which words are palindromes.

Palindromic words are those that read the same forward and backward, such as wow, solos, tenet, or rotator.

Madam, did you refer to the sagas of Bob and Anna, the civic planner and the racecar driver?
